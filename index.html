Virtual tour of an 3D interior space with VR

You can experience furniture layouts, finishes, lighting, and décor in real scale. This immersive tour allows you to suggest changes such as moving furniture, adjusting colors, or swapping materials in real time, ensuring the design perfectly matches your taste before construction or installation begins.

Bringing Architectural Elevations to Life in 3D

Experience a life-size 3D elevation of your building projected onto the actual location. This immersive view allows you to walk around the design, see architectural details, materials, and finishes in realistic scale, and understand how the structure will look in its real surroundings well before construction is complete. It helps you make confident design choices and visualize the final outcome with unmatched clarity.

See Your Floor Plan, Wiring & Plumbing Come to Life in VR

On the construction site, you can wear a VR headset to view a full-scale 2D floor plan. The virtual display highlights detailed electrical layouts, including wiring routes, switch points, and lighting positions, as well as plumbing designs showing pipelines, fixtures, and drainage paths. This immersive visualization lets you walk through your project in real time, understand technical layouts before installation, and request adjustments with precision - reducing errors, saving costs, and improving decision-making.
